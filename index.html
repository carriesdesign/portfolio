About Me

BORN ON THE 8TH JUNE 2000, I AM A TRAINED MEDICAL ARTIST (MSC MEDICAL VISUALISATION AND HUMAN ANATOMY) WITH EXPERIENCE IN PHOTOGRAPHY, DIGITAL DESIGN, VISUAL EFFECTS AND 3D MODELLING.

Carrie, Digital Designer and Photographer

Hey there, I'm Carrie,...

... a 23 year old artist who currently finished her postgraduate degree at the Glasgow School of Art in Visualization. I am German living since 5 years in the UK where I studied and used to work as a Graffiti Artist. In my free time I love to sketch, paint, gel print and edit photos. Through my degree I found my passion to photography, digital design and 3D art. I love photorealistic design, colour lumteri and motion graphics. Colour correction, Lighting and Glitch effects is what I live for. My style tends towards a grunge and retro design however, I can never resist the beauty of minimal and aesthetic design for long.
